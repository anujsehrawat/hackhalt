HACKHALT

Welcome to "Hack Halt," an electrifying cybersecurity festival hosted by PIET (Panipat Institute of Engineering and Technology) and organised by team ACCESS DENIED on the 29th and 30th of November. This event is not just a celebration of cybersecurity; it's a tribute to National Computer Security Day, and it's designed to empower and inspire enthusiasts, professionals, and students alike.
